About Us

Vokal is an all-in-one presentation software that assists with improving all the different components of a great presentation. The software utilizes voice transcription, body motion tracking and sentiment analysis to provide real-time feedback for improvements.

Realtime Feedback

From filler words to long pauses, Vokal will identify points that make your presentation less effective.

Live Statistics

With live statistics, it will help the user understand their pace (Words per Minute), volume and the amount of filler words they are using.

Instant Turnaround

Our platform provides instant transcriptions on your presentation for quick and convenient changes.
